Why Ghana?

Ghana, The Gold Coast Country

Ghana was formerly called 'The Gold Coast' until it gained its independence in 1957 from the British. The independence movement was led by Kwame Nkrumah. It was called The Gold Coast because of the large supply of gold that the country has.

The capital of Ghana is Accra but there are 16 regions all with their own capitals. Ghana is a peaceful country with lovely people and beautiful culture. When you visit the country, you can see the rich culture everywhere you go. There are a variety of foods to try whether on the streets or in the restaurants. There are also beautiful art museums to visit.

A few places to check out

Kakum National Park

For the nature lovers this is a great place to visit. It also allows you to see other parts of Ghana apart from Accra.

Address:

R82, Ghana

About this place

The Kakum National Park is located in the Central region of Ghana in Cape Coast. It has various activities that you can participate in. Its known for its canopy walks. They have 7 suspension bridge that can hang up to 27 meters.

National Theatre

A day basking in the theatre and enjoying locally produced plays and movies.

Address:

South Liberia Road, Accra, Ghana

About this place

The National Theatre is a great place to visit for people who enjoy the arts. At the theatre they perform various plays and movies produced and directed by Ghanians. The theatre is a good place to have a chilled day.

Learn more

The Royal Senchi

A good resort to get away from the busy city. Enjoy the nature and some peace and quiet!

Address:

Senchi Ferry Road, Akosombo, Ghana

About this place

The Royal Senchi is located in Akosombo in the Eastern Region of Ghana. The resort is on the banks of the Volta River. You can spend a day at the resort or spend a weekend there. They have a variety of activites to do and you can also see a variety of animals at the nature park.
